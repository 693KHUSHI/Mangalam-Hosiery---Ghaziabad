Who We Are

From Our Family to Yours

Our journey in the hosiery and garment business began over 20 years ago with a simple vision: to provide every family in our neighbourhood with quality garments at honest prices. Mangalam Hosiery was established a few years ago as a continuation of this long-standing experience, built on the same values of trust and quality.

We are a family-run business. Every customer who walks through our door is treated like a neighbour, not just a sale. We take the time to understand your needs, recommend the right products, and ensure you leave satisfied every time.

Today, we stock over 10 leading brands and a wide range of product varieties for men, women, and children. Whether you're looking for a single pair of socks or placing a bulk wholesale order, we offer the same care, transparency, and personal attention.
